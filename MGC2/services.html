Our Services

Comprehensive anti-corruption solutions tailored to your organization's needs.

At Minenhle Global Consulting, we offer a full spectrum of anti-corruption consulting services designed to protect your organization, ensure compliance with global regulations, and foster an ethical business culture. Each of our services can be customized to address your specific industry challenges and organizational structure.

Anti-Corruption Risk Assessments

Our thorough corruption risk assessments identify vulnerabilities within your organization before they become problems. We analyze your operations, third-party relationships, and geographic footprint to pinpoint specific corruption risks.

What We Deliver:

  • Comprehensive risk mapping across all business units
  • Evaluation of third-party relationships and supply chains
  • Country and sector-specific risk profiling
  • Detailed risk mitigation recommendations
  • Benchmarking against industry standards

Compliance Framework Design

We design and implement robust anti-corruption compliance frameworks aligned with international standards including FCPA, UK Bribery Act, and ISO 37001. Our frameworks provide the structure needed to prevent, detect, and respond to corruption risks.

What We Deliver:

  • Anti-corruption policies and procedures
  • Compliance management systems
  • Due diligence protocols
  • Monitoring and evaluation mechanisms
  • ISO 37001 implementation support

Policy & Process Audits

Our audit services evaluate the effectiveness of your existing anti-corruption policies and processes. We identify gaps, inefficiencies, and opportunities for improvement to strengthen your compliance posture.

What We Deliver:

  • Compliance program assessment against global standards
  • Policy effectiveness reviews
  • Process efficiency evaluations
  • Control testing and validation
  • Detailed remediation plans

Ethics and Anti-Bribery Training

We deliver engaging, interactive training programs that equip your employees with the knowledge and skills to recognize corruption risks and make ethical decisions. Our training can be delivered in-person or through digital platforms.

What We Deliver:

  • Customized training modules for various employee levels
  • Interactive workshops and scenario-based learning
  • Train-the-trainer programs

Whistleblower Program Support

We help you develop and implement effective whistleblower programs that encourage reporting of potential violations while protecting those who come forward. Our solutions comply with the EU Whistleblower Directive and other global standards.

What We Deliver:

  • Whistleblower system design and implementation

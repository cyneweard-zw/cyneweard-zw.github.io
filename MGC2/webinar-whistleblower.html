How the Corrupt Use Legal Tactics to Retaliate Against Whistleblowers

Webinar Recording

In this enlightening webinar, experts discuss how corrupt entities use legal tactics to intimidate and silence whistleblowers. Our panel explores real-world case studies and provides practical guidance on how whistleblowers can protect themselves while exposing wrongdoing.

Key Topics Covered

  • Strategic lawsuits against public participation (SLAPPs) and how they're used to intimidate whistleblowers
  • Common legal threats used to silence those who expose corruption
  • The financial and psychological impact of legal retaliation
  • Practical steps whistleblowers can take to protect themselves
  • How organizations can support whistleblowers against retaliatory legal action

About This Webinar

This webinar was originally hosted by the Platform to Protect Whistleblowers in Africa (PPLAAF) on April 25, 2023. We're sharing it as part of our commitment to supporting whistleblower protection worldwide.
